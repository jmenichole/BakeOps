## 1. Business Overview

### 1.1 Company Description

BakeBuilder is a comprehensive web platform that helps professional cake bakers:
- Generate AI mockups using their actual cake photos (realistic, not generic)
- Automate pricing with customizable rules
- Plan multi-day production with component timing (fondant, flowers, etc.)
- Accept bookings and payments through integrated Stripe
- Build community through galleries, forums, and social features

### 1.2 Problem Statement

**Baker Pain Points** (validated by Mom's experience):

1. **Time-Consuming Admin Work**
   - 3-5 hours per cake just on quotes and communication
   - Manual scheduling across multiple orders
   - Tracking ingredients across orders
   - Result: Less time for actual baking

2. **Difficulty Showing Mockups**
   - Photos of past work don't match customer's vision
   - Sketches aren't professional enough
   - Generic AI tools create unrealistic cakes
   - Result: Lost sales, miscommunication

3. **Complex Production Planning**
   - Fondant needs 2-3 days prep time
   - Gum paste flowers need 3-5 days to dry
   - Must coordinate multiple cakes simultaneously
   - Calculating batch ingredients by color
   - Result: Stress, mistakes, inefficiency

4. **No Integrated Business System**
   - Using separate tools for quotes, scheduling, payments
   - No automated reminders or confirmations
   - Result: Unprofessional experience, payment delays

### 1.3 Solution

BakeBuilder provides an all-in-one platform:

**Core Features**:
- AI mockup generation trained on baker's actual work
- Automated pricing engine with custom rules
- Multi-day production planner with prep tips
- Batch ingredient calculator (e.g., "3.5 lbs pink fondant across 3 orders")
- Customer booking portal with Stripe payments
- Weekly printable planner for production

**Phase 2 Features** (Year 2):
- Baker gallery for customer inspiration browsing
- Community forum for baker-to-baker support
- Social feed for sharing work and techniques
- Customer-baker matching algorithm

### 1.4 Competitive Advantage

**vs. Square Appointments** ($50/month):
- They have: Basic booking and calendar
- We have: AI mockups + production planning + pricing automation
- **Our advantage**: Baker-specific, saves 10+ hours/week

**vs. GlossGenius** ($24/month):
- They have: Beauty industry focus, basic booking
- We have: Custom cake specialization, AI tools, production planning
- **Our advantage**: Built by bakers for bakers

**vs. Generic AI Tools**:
- They create: Unrealistic cakes bakers can't actually make
- We create: Mockups trained on YOUR actual work
- **Our advantage**: Realistic, bookable designs

**Our Unique Position**: Only platform combining AI mockups + production planning + booking + community specifically for cake bakers.

- *Mitigation*:

  - Mom's baker network loyalty
  - Community features create moat
  - Constant innovation

**Risk 5: Family Dynamics Cause Conflict**

- *Impact*: High

  - Clear roles and responsibilities

**Risk 6: Technical Challenges/Delays**
- *Probability*: Medium
