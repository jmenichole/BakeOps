BOT — Baked On Time

AI-powered workflow for custom cake businesses.

  • Instant designs from customer ideas
  • Accurate quoting & booking
  • Ingredient & batch planner
  • Production timeline dashboard

Problem

  • Missed conversions due to delay

Solution

  • Automated quoting tied to design attributes
  • Live dashboard with prep status bars
