BOT Founding Family Business Plan

Collaborative operating blueprint for Angela (CPO), Jess (CFO/HR), and Jme (CTO).
Mission: Empower custom cake bakers to grow profitable businesses by automating admin work, showcasing artistry through AI, and connecting them with customers.

The Opportunity

The custom cake industry (>$10B) includes tens of thousands of professionals who lose hours weekly to manual quoting, planning, and communication. They lack realistic mockups, unified scheduling, and integrated pricing + payment flows. BOT consolidates these into one specialized, baker-focused platform.

  • 10–15 admin hours/week per baker → reclaimed via automation
  • AI mockups increase close rate & clarity
  • Production planner reduces stress & mistakes
  • Integrated booking elevates professionalism

Family Team Structure

Angela – CPO Jess – CFO/HR Jme – CTO

Complementary roles create a resilient, capital‑efficient founding team anchored in authentic baker insight, disciplined financial stewardship, and strong technical execution.

  • Angela (CPO): Baker needs expert, quality validation, user research.
  • Jess (CFO/HR): Financial modeling, cash flow, HR policies, compliance, support ops.
  • Jme (CTO): Architecture, product build, AI integration, technical strategy.

Why Family Works

Core Product Phasing

Phase 1 (Months 1‑6)

  • AI mockup (generic model)
  • Cake configurator & pricing engine
  • Booking + deposit flow (Stripe)
  • Basic order dashboard

Phase 2 (Months 7‑12)

  • Custom LoRA style training
  • Production planner & batch ingredient calculator
  • Calendar automation + reminders
  • Printable weekly production schedule

Phase 3 (Year 2)

  • Baker gallery & inspiration requests
  • Community feed + forum
  • Customer‑baker matching algorithm

Pricing Strategy

  • 7‑day frictionless trial (no credit card) limited to 2 orders.
  • $49/mo Premium: unlimited orders, AI training, planner.
  • $470/yr (20% discount) + onboarding session.
  • Target LTV $900, CAC $150‑250 (3.6:1 ratio).

Go‑to‑Market

Phase 1 Beta

  • Mom’s network (10‑20 bakers), testimonials, iteration loop.
  • Collect usage metrics → refine messaging.

Phase 2 Community & Local

  • Facebook baker groups, Instagram reels (mockup transformations).
  • Early referral incentive (1 free month per referral).

Phase 3 Paid Acquisition

  • Targeted ads (pricing calculators as lead magnets).
  • YouTube tutorials & blog SEO (“how to price custom cakes”).
  • Influencer collabs (decorating educators).

Financial & Costs

Year 1 Investment ≈ $75K (includes living stipends + modest marketing). Break‑even at ~153 monthly equivalent subscribers. Flexible funding paths: bootstrap, family pool, loan, small angel.

Key Risks & Mitigation

  • Adoption: Leverage authentic baker network → beta trust.
  • AI Quality: Iterative improvement; fallback manual editing.
  • Payments: Stripe reliability; finance oversight.
  • Competition: Speed + niche depth + community moat.
  • Family Conflict: Clear role docs + weekly meeting cadence.
  • Technical Delays: MVP scoping + agile iteration + buffer time.
