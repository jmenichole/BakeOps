BOT — Baked On Time

Mission: Compress the custom cake workflow from multi-day manual coordination into a guided, automated, AI-assisted flow.

Problem

  • Slow quoting → lost conversions
  • Unstructured customer inputs
  • Manual ingredient + batch planning
  • Operational blind spots between prep phases

Solution Pillars

  • AI design generation (portfolio-trained)
  • Automated pricing + booking link
  • Live production timeline dashboard
  • Ingredient batch consolidation

Market

Beachhead: 8–10K digitally active custom cake studios (US/CA). Expansion to weddings, dessert artistry, confection verticals.

Business Model

  • SaaS subscription tiers
  • Booking transaction fee
  • Design credit packs
  • Supply marketplace (phase 3)

Roadmap Highlights

  • Beta: design + quoting accuracy
  • Dashboard & batch planner
  • Payments + timeline automation
  • Marketplace pilot

Competitive Edge

Closed loop: inspiration → realistic design → production plan → delivery tracking. Others stop at design or inventory.

Pre-Seed Ask

$450K to reach post-beta traction and AI differentiation milestones.
